Mariah Pineda is an experienced director, financial manager, and stock trader. After completeing her bachelors in 2024, she will attend graduate school to pursue a masters in business administration. She plans to open her open real estate brokerage and a nonprofit learning center for underserved hispanic students.

Born and raised in California, Mariah received her high school diploma from Redlands East Valley High School. She is currently attending the University of California- Riverside as a Pre- Business student in the College of Humanities, Arts, and Social Sciences. Her experience in leadership range from soccer captain to National Honors Society President. She is currently a member of Biomedical Ethics Club, Teach IE, and the Association of Latino Professionals for America. Education and community service are vitally important to her, especially when serving the younger generations because she believes that our future lies in the hands of the children.
